## üîë Key Concept: What is Deep Research?

**Deep Research** = An AI mode that searches the internet, reads multiple sources, and synthesizes information into a comprehensive report.

### Regular Chat vs. Deep Research

| Regular AI Chat | Deep Research Mode |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Answers from AI's training data | Actively searches the internet |
| Quick responses (seconds) | Takes time to research (1-3 minutes) |
| Good for simple questions | Good for complex topics |
| May be outdated | Finds current information |
| Single perspective | Multiple sources synthesized |

### When to Use Deep Research

| Use Deep Research For | Use Regular Chat For |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| Planning trips or events | Quick facts or definitions |
| Comparing products/options | Creative writing |
| Current events research | Code generation |
| Finding local businesses | Explaining concepts |
| Complex multi-step planning | Simple questions |

### Step 2: Start Deep Research

1. Open **Google Gemini** at [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)
2. Look for Deep Research mode:
   - Click the model selector (usually says "Gemini" at top)
   - Select "Deep Research" or "1.5 Pro with Deep Research"
   - If you don't see it, you may need Google AI Pro

### Step 3: Use the Magic Prompt

**Example: Tokyo Disneyland Trip** (copy & paste):
```
I'm a student planning a dream trip to Tokyo Disneyland from my city.
Please research and create a complete 5-day trip plan including:

1. GETTING THERE
   - Best airports to fly into
   - Average flight costs
   - Best time of year to visit Tokyo Disneyland

2. WHERE TO STAY
   - 3 hotel options at different price points near Tokyo Disneyland
   - Which Disney resort hotels are available
   - Is it better to stay on-site or nearby?

3. WHAT TO DO
   - Must-do attractions at Tokyo Disneyland
   - Must-do attractions at Tokyo DisneySea
   - Best activities for teenagers
   - Any special events or seasonal attractions

4. FOOD
   - Best restaurants inside the parks
   - Local Japanese foods I must try
   - Any food allergy considerations

5. PRACTICAL INFO
   - Do I need a passport or visa for Japan?
   - How to buy park tickets in advance
   - What's the local currency and should I exchange money?
   - Useful Japanese phrases
   - How to get from the airport to the resort

Please include specific names, prices in USD, and current information.
```

**Want a different destination? Use this template:**
```
I'm a student planning a dream trip to [YOUR DESTINATION] from [YOUR CITY].
Please research and create a complete 5-day trip plan including:

1. GETTING THERE
```

### Step 4: Watch AI Research

While Deep Research runs, notice:
- AI shows what it's searching for
- Multiple sources are being checked

- This takes longer than regular chat (1-3 minutes)

**Discussion while waiting:** Why do you think this takes longer than a regular AI response?

## üìö Key Vocabulary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Deep Research** | AI mode that searches the internet and synthesizes multiple sources |
| **Synthesis** | Combining information from multiple sources into one coherent summary |
| **Source** | The original website or document where information came from |
| **Verification** | Checking if information is accurate by consulting original sources |
| **Iteration** | Asking follow-up questions to get more detailed information |
| **Primary Source** | Original, firsthand information (official websites, news reports) |
| **Secondary Source** | Information summarized or interpreted from primary sources |
